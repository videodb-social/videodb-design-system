Cards

Three families.
Soft, charcoal, charcoal-active.

Soft cards stay quiet in the editorial flow. Charcoal cards land inside light sections as featured content. The active state — orange ring, glow, four-pixel lift — is reserved for tier selection and featured picks.

01 · Soft card
Editorial surface

Inline content card on light sections. Background #E8E8EA, radius 12px, padding 24px, hover #DCDCDE.

02 · Charcoal card
Dark featured

Charcoal #161616 inside a light section — not #0A0A0A. The lift relative to the F5 background is what creates the layering without a shadow.

03 · Active state
Selected tier

Orange ring + soft glow + 4px lift. Inactive siblings drop to 0.55 opacity to reinforce focus.
